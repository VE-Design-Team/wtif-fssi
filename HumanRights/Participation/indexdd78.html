PANEL Definition

  • Participation

  • Accountability

  • Empowerment

Participation is the right to take part in making active, free and meaningful decisions that affect our lives. Service users may need help from support workers to fully participate. This may mean helping them get access to information, understanding it and communicating what they think to the people involved in their care, to make the best possible decisions.
