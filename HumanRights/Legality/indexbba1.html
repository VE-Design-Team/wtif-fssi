There are a great many laws that protect and promote human rights. Support services have to develop policies and procedures that allow workers to implement human rights-based practice. They should also train staff in how to follow the policies and procedures.

It is important to know who to go to with a complaint. The first step is your supervisor or to someone more senior in the organisation. If this doesn’t work, you may choose to go to an external regulator or advocacy organisation. You should also seek support from your union.
