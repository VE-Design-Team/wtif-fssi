Â The first step to achieving equality is to prohibit and prevent discrimination. Discrimination is the unfair or harmful treatment of people based on characteristic such as their age, abilities, beliefs or background. These characteristics are known as identity markers. Identity markers are ways that we define and describe different groups of people.

Living a life free of discrimination is a human right.
