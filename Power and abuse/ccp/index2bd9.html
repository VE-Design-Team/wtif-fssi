Culture

Sometimes the most troubling cases of power and abuse involve whole communities. Why do local communities strongly defend industriesÂ like coal mining when we know that this sort of work is harmful to health and well-being?

How do communities end up treating people with disability or the elderly very badly over long periods of time, locking them in institutions, providing them with low or no income, and ensuring they are very much at the bottom of the social order?

On the flip side, how is it that some communities manage to treat those with disability or the elderly very well? What has enabled them to be so enlightened and who should take credit for this?

This raises the important issue of culture and where people acquire their values and beliefs.
