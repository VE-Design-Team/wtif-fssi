Financial abuse

Financial abuse is the improper or illegal use of a personâ€™s finances or property. This type of abuse can happen in one of two ways. The first is when a person is being threatened or coerced by someone they know and trust, while the second is where the abuser is unknowingly being taken advantage of.

It is important to know how a person is being financially abused because each type offers different signs of abuse and ways to respond. For example, a threatened person may not be willing to speak with the police because of the possible danger that could result.

On the other hand, a person who is unaware may seem confused when questioned by police, and their actions could be misinterpreted as lying.
