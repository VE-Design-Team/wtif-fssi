Neglect

Select the images to learn some of the signs of neglect or use the arrows to move through the images.

Signs associated with a lack of food, water, and basic hygiene care include:

  • malnutrition and dehydration, with symptoms of fatigue, weakness, dizziness, and mental confusion
  • excessive weight loss
  • skin rashes caused by fungal, bacterial, or viral infections smelliness and dirty clothes.

Sign from a lack of mental or physical stimulation:

  • listlessness, having low energy and taking little interest in things around them, withdrawal, not wanting to participate in activities or engage with others
  • loneliness or depression from having little to no social contact.

Signs of insufficient or inappropriate medical care:

  • ongoing and unresolved illnesses or injuries
  • grogginess, sleepiness, or pain from being under or over-medicated.

Signs related to being exposed to unsafe situations:

  • accidents and injuries
  • anxiety, stress, and fear
  • hypothermia or overheating due to inappropriate clothing or environment.
