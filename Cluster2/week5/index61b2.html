Introduction

In this module, you will cover the following learning objectives and key activities.

Select each of the icons for more information about the learning objectives and key activities relating to this module.
  • Learning objectives
  • Key activities

At the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • list the most common positive lifestyle enhancement strategies
  • define positive reinforcement
  • explain what SMART goals are
  • describe the most common stress management techniques
  • explain why engaging in meaningful activities is important
  • describe the three main purposes of support relationships
  • list the difficulties people with disabilities face with nutrition
  • identify the three attributes that need to be considered for improving environments.
  • Identify barriers people with disability face and what support workers can do to address them.
  • Recognise how environments can be modified to support people with disability.
  • Consider cultural differences when applying positive lifestyle enhancement strategies.
