Workplace practice

As a support worker, you may be employed in a variety of settings. Your work and range of roles may lead you to work in the health sector, social services industry,Â justice areas, etc. for a broad number of employers or organisations. Regardless of your role, context, or organisation, working with difference and diversity is a key element of modern human service work.

In order to promote inclusion and accommodate diversity, it is important to be aware of the principles of good work practice as an individual and from an organisational perspective.
