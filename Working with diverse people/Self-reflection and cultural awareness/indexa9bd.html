Cultural diversity

Cultural diversity refers to the variety of cultures within a society.

Cultural awareness

Cultural awareness involves developing a sensitivity to and understanding of, another culture. This usually involves thinking about your own culture, beliefs and values.

Cultural competence

Cultural competence is how we use our sensitivity and knowledge to communicate effectively with people from different cultures.
