Embracing diversity and professional growth

Work through the following scenario to embrace diversity and professional growth. This is not an assessment. In each step, select the action you would take. You can try this activity as many times as you like.

A gay couple in a long term relationship move into the aged care facility where you work.
You don’t approve of same sex couples as it is against your religion.

  • You ask not to support them.
  • You tell the couple that they will be punished for their sinful choices.

You have successfully used self-reflection and embraced the opportunity to grow personally and professionally.
