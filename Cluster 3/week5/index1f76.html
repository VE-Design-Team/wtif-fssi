Experiences of ageing: from a resident

Watch the following video to hear the experiences of an aged care resident, Dot. Pay particular attention to her experience of loss and grief, her social interactions and how they affect her well-being and her recognition of how her dementia impacts her. While watching and listening to Dot, think about the risk factors that may be present for her and also the protective factors around her.
