Effective communication strategies

The following are communication strategies that encourage interaction and improve quality of life for people with dementia that you support:

  • acknowledge their feelings
  • allow the person to express distress (and provide them with support)
  • provide verbal and/or nonverbal reassurance
  • use reminiscence frequently to connect with clients.
