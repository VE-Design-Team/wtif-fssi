Re-ablement strategies

Re-ablement is support that promotes resident independence, assists them to maintain and/or strengthen their capacity to undertake daily activities, and allows them to maintain social and community connections.

Select the checkboxes to see what re-ablement strategies and actions include;
  • Communicating effectively with residents to understand their needs and wants.
  • Empowering residents to re-train their bodies to carry out safe tasks and activities they could no longer do independently because of illness or ageing (such as brushing their teeth).
  • Encouraging residents to move their bodies and exercise; if not actively, then at least passively.
