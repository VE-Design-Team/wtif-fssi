Myths and facts about mental illness

To effectively identify behaviours of concern, you will need to be aware of the myths and facts about mental illness.

Widely held false beliefs or ideas are myths. These add to stigma (an experience of being considered different from others) and make life harder for people affected by mental illness. While society has a much better understanding and awareness of mental illness now, many sufferers still feel their illness is viewed as a weakness or a character flaw instead of a genuine health condition.
