The digital divide

Technology has the ability to improve everyoneâ€™s lives, but not everyone has equal access to it. This gap in accessibility is known as the digital divide.

The lack in accessibility is mainly due to the high costs associated with technology. Older people and people with disability who have limited resources may not have the finances to purchase or access the technology they need.

Systems for funding technology for older people and people with disabilities tend to divide people further by classifying individuals according to various categories and levels of disability to determine who is most in need. We will explore this concept in more detail throughout the course.
