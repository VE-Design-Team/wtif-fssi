Identifying levels of support

For the following scenarios, consider what level of support you should provide for the service user.

Ramona has quadriplegia, a type of cerebral palsy which has no effect on her cognitive abilities but severely limits her posture, balance, and movement. She has little to no control of the muscles in her body. What level of support does she require for meal assistance?
