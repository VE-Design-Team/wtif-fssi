Diversity terms and concepts

As a service worker, you will engage with people from a diverse range of communities and groups. This includes the service users and their families, your co-workers and with management. Â 

Australia is made up of many different types of people and in your role as a service worker you can expect to come in contact with:
  • Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders
  • People with disabilities, including those experiencing mental health issues
  • Older people
  • Young people
  • People with diverse genders and sexualities
  • People from many different cultural, linguistic and faith communities
