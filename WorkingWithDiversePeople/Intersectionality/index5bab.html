Respecting others

In your role, you will interact with a range of people, including managers, supervisors, services users and people from other organisations. You will need to be aware of, consider and respect that people you come across will have different cultures, beliefs and ideas to you. When communicating with others, take the time to listen and understand things from their perspective.

Select each of the check boxes to reveal how people can differ based on their cultural backgrounds, beliefs, and ideas.
  • History, traditions, and family systems.
  • Behaviours, attitudes, and values.
  • Language, speech patterns, and communication styles.
  • Social, environmental, health and organisational policies.
