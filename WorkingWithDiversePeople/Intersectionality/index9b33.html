Applying intersectionality

 An intersectional approach promotes communication, respect and understanding between people. However, if having a conversation is not possible or unsuccessful, you can refer the matter to your supervisor as they have legal obligations to resolve the matter.

In more severe cases, discrimination complaints can be made to the Australian Human Rights Commission or the relevant anti-discrimination or equal opportunity commissions in your state, such as the Victorian Equal Opportunity Human Rights Commission (VEOHRC). If the discrimination is in the form of violent or criminal behaviour, contact police immediately by calling 000.
