Introduction

In this module, you will cover the following learning objectives, key activities and assessment.

Select each of the icons for more information about the learning objectives, key activities and assessments relating to this module.
  • Learning objectives
  • Key activities

At the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • participate in self-reflective activities to seek a better understanding of self
  • work with individuals, groups and communities in a manner that reflects differing values, cultures and practices
  • promote equality and support inclusion through work activities and behaviour
  • apply cultural safety principles.
  • Self reflection activity.

There are no assessable tasks in this Module. However, concepts and skills are developed that feed into Assessment task 1-2.
