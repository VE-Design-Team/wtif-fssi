The ableist way

What do we see when we look at another person? We see things in a particular way because of how we are taught to see them. If we are taught that people with disability are lesser humans who are sad, dependent and in need of constant supervision or need to be subject to the rules of ‘better’ humans, we will act in an ableist way.
